All preprocessing fixes applied

Player → team aggregation (Anthony): Groups all players on each roster per season and averages their stats to produce one row per team. Required so the model compares two teams head-to-head.
Playoff matchup results table (Anthony): Hardcoded all 180 NBA playoff series from 2012–2024 (winner, loser, round). The CSVs only showed which players were in the playoffs — not who played who or who won.
Data leakage fix (Benjamin): Original stats used full end-of-season averages — a leak, since those numbers include games played after the playoffs started. Benjamin fixed this by computing each team's stats only from games played before the playoff series.
Dataset doubling: Each series becomes two rows (winner's perspective + loser's perspective) so the model sees balanced examples. Same approach as March ML Mania.
